The "Senagat Plastic" is a manufacturer of high quality plastic pallets in Turkmenistan and of various sizes.

About our company

The plant for the production of plastic pallets of the economic society "Senagat Plastic" was built in the period 2017-2018. In order to ensure the tasks of diversification and import substitution of products coming from other countries, as well as the possibility of entering the international market of competitive products produced in Turkmenistan.

Methods for the production of plastic pallets

When you choosing plastic pallets very important to understand which process was used for pallet manufacturing, and which quality raw material was used for manufacturing. Our pallets are made by injection molding process, using equipment manufactured in the:
- Injection molding
- Press casting
- Rotational molding
United States, and are characterized by high performance, because this method ensures the homogenization of the melt, and uniform distribution of the material in the mold during injection under an overpressure of 4000- 5000PSI

Features

Product quality

Our pallets are produced by injection molding using equipment manufactured in the United States, and are characterized by high performance, because this method provides homogenization of the melt, and uniform distribution of the material in the form of injection under overpressure 4000-5000PSI.

Using

Construction and petrochemical industry for transportation of materials and chemically aggressive substances-cement, paints, varnishes, chemicals, etc

Meat, dairy, fish and other food industry for delivery of packaged food

Medical industry for transportation and temporary storage of goods requiring special hygienic conditions

Преимущества

Appearance

Not subject to shrinkage, cracking and rotting

Resistance to chemically aggressive and caustic substances
