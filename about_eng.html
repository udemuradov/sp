History of the company

The plant for the production of plastic pallets of the economic society "Senagat Plastic" was built in the period 2017-2018. In order to ensure the tasks of diversification and import substitution of products coming from other countries, as well as the possibility of entering the international market of competitive products produced in Turkmenistan.
Construction and commissioning of the facility was completed within 12 months, and in 2018 the facility received the necessary permits and was put into commercial operation with a production capacity of 240,000 pallets per year.
In 2019, the production capacity was expanded, which allowed to increase production to 480,000 pallets per year, and to expand the range of products.

The plant is located in close proximity to the International sea port of Turkmenbashi, intercity railway and trunk roads, which allows to ship products in the fastest possible time and provides opportunities for international logistics.

The economic society "Senagat Plastic", and its production, is certified according to the local legislation, and also has the international certificates ISO 9001-2015, ISO 14001-2015, OHSAS 18001-2007

Currently, ES "Senagat Plastic" produces several types of single-deck plastic pallets different sizes and different static and dynamic load capacity.
